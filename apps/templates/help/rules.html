These are the rules of Kwissle - the real-time general knowledge quiz battle

  • You play against an opponent in real-time
  • You are each shown the same question at the same time (10 questions in total)
  • If you answer correctly, and faster than the opponent, you win!
    • 3 points if you know and type in the answer
    • 1 point if you click to load alternatives and get it right
    • 0 points if your opponent answers before you.
  • Whoever has the most points at the end of the round wins the battle
