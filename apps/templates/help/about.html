Technology

This web app was written in Tornado Python web framework and tornadio and socket.io for the actual game part.

You'll need a modern browser to play - Chrome and Safari work best, and if you're using Firefox, you'll need Flash enabled.

About

Kwissle is one of the first real-time general knowledge quiz games of its kind on the web. It uses bleeding edge technology to pit your wits against real opponents in a contest of knowledge and speed.

Our goal is to have lots of people playing, having fun and learning something. To make that possible, the site needs to employ some clever game metrics to make it worthwhile to come back and play more. Also, for lots of people to be able to play a lot we need lots of questions. All questions are crowd source, i.e. by you.

Anybody can add a question and everybody is encouraged to review other peoples questions so that ultimately your contributed questions can be published by the administrator.
More info about the question workflow available here.

Kwissle was written and maintained by Peter Bengtsson
