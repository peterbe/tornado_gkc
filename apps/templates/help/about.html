About

Kwissle was written and maintained by Peter Bengtsson

It's goal is to have lots of people playing, having fun and learning something. To make that possible, the site needs to employ some clever game metrics to make it worthwhile to come back and play more. Also, for lots of people to be able to play a lot we need lots of questions. All questions are crowd source, i.e. by you.

Anybody can add a question and everybody is encouraged to review other peoples questions so that ultimately your contributed questions can be published by the administrator.
More info about the question workflow available here.
